Whether a party will be a success or a massive flop depends on the early crowd. Are they just coming for a free shot and leaving or are they staying and heating up the dance floor? For token developers the management of airdrops grants many advantages:

First, their token becomes more stable. The Freibier.io system is rewarding token holders depending on the time and the number of tokens they hold (among other factors). Gone are the days of immediate fluctuation right after people have received their airdrops. 

Furthermore, Freibier.io offers a set of features to run and organize the airdrops intelligently: After setting a budget of token for all airdrops, our system does the rest: Batch calculating individual results, batch sending, batch notifications and batch rewarding. 

To increase the popularity of their token, token developers receive a big boost in reach, as all CERVEZA holders will be notified of their launch immediately. Token developers receive access to a high-quality crowd that is ready to buy and hold tokens. 

And with airdrops being automated and optimized token developers have more free time for delivering a fantastic project.

What's with the name "Freibier"

Freibier is everyone's favorite word in Germany. It translates to "free beer". Since we're a German development team, we love beer, especially when it's free. How is it connected to this project? All CERVEZA ($CVZA) holders will get more beer in the form of CERVEZA ($CVZA) over time depending on how long and how many CERVEZA tokens they hold. Since free beer is why everyone is here, we thought we could call it free beer to begin with.
