What is the brewery doing?

... brewing beer of course! The brewery is our version of a liquidity farm. Yield farming, also known as liquidity mining, is one way of earning passive income with cryptocurrencies. Simply put, it means keeping cryptocurrencies locked and receiving rewards for doing so. In a way, yield farming can be compared to staking, but it is a bit more complex in the background. Our goal is to simplify this process and thus give everyone access to free beer... or free money! In order to receive rewards from the brewery, we ask you to support our liquidity pool. This is basically a smart contract that contains funds that enable trading $CVZA. In return for providing liquidity for the pool, you will receive a reward in the form of liquidity token.

Why is it important to be early to the brewery?

The rewards distributed are subject to certain conditions, but in general, the entire reward pool is divided by the number of liquidity providers. This means that you will receive a lot of free liquidity tokens as a reward when becoming a brewer early, before others.  This will make brewing beer extremely profitable for you with a possible APY of up to 10000%!

The earlier you start brewing, the higher your rewards.

How can I get in early?

We’ve set up a waiting list for early access to the brewery and you can sign up here. After the registration, you will receive a link with which you can promote the brewery. Every new brewer hired results in a promotion for you. In other words: the more brewers you recruit, the sooner you will have access to the brewery before others and profit from much higher daily payouts!
